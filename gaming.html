Gaming Info

Gaming can be great fun! Or it can be totally serious. Sometimes both at the same time.

So I've put together a list of my top tips for great gamers

Make a to-do list

A game to-do list is a list of what you want to do in a game.

For example: Minecraft

In Minecraft, you always will come to a point were you will say"What will I do next?"

So this is why you should do a to-do list

Don't forget your michrophone!

If you are a quiet talker then it would be better if you have a michrophone.

Here are some reasons of why you should always have a michrophone:

    • For your viewers to hear you loud and clear.

    • So if you are shy it will sound like you were never shy.

    Plan were your Studio/Office will be

    When you are filming it is always better if you film in a studio or office

    You should also plan which room will be your office or studio.

    If it is a Studio then I think the best room is the biggest room

    If it is a Office then I think the best room is at least the 2nd biggest or 3rd

    Know when you are on

    When you are going to film a video you should plan when you want to start the video.

    For me that is a really good idea. And I think everyone agrees, right?

    If you keep losing when you are fighting a boss, don't give up - think of a plan to defeat the boss!!!
